Chapter 4

CRR Strategy and Program Framework

4.1 Strategic Alignment

Community Risk Reduction programs at DFD are directly aligned with the Department Strategic Plan. Two goals specifically govern CRR operations:

Goal 3: Develop Community Risk Reduction Plan following NFPA 1300 — Align CRR programs with national standards, document risk assessment methodology, and track intervention effectiveness.

Goal 4: Conduct thorough review of Public Education programs in line with Standards of Cover/CPSE — Evaluate program reach and effectiveness, document outcomes and metrics, and maintain a continuous improvement cycle.

Division Objectives: Evaluate and analyze data to reduce risk in the community through education. Recruit and retain a diverse workforce.

4.2 Target Hazard-Based Approach

Beginning in FY 25-26, the CRR Division implemented a data-driven approach to public education programming. All content is directly tied to identified community hazards through two primary sources:

  • Incident investigation data from the Fire Marshal’s Office
  • Nationally recognized seasonal and emerging hazard trends

This approach ensures that every piece of public education content addresses real, documented risks rather than generic safety messaging. The monthly CRR Newsletter program was specifically designed around this target hazard analysis framework.

4.4 Metrics Framework

The CRR Division tracks performance through the following key indicators. These metrics feed into quarterly Program Appraisals and accreditation documentation.

Community Outreach Metrics

  • Events delivered per quarter (Q1 FY 25-26 baseline: 63\)
  • Community members reached per quarter
  • Events by category: fire truck presentations, NNO, tours, public events, presentations, parades, detector installs
  • Hunter attended (Yes/No) tracked per event

Newsletter Metrics

  • Subscriber count (CRR: 471, Recruiting: 3,960 as of Q1)
  • Open rate (target: 25%+)
  • Click-through rate
  • Unsubscribe rate

Technology Tool Metrics

  • Water Shutoff Survey submissions
  • Fire Watch guidelines accessed
  • Knox Box guide views
  • After the Fire page visits

Data Sources

  • ESO Community Events dashboard — primary event tracking system
  • Constant Contact analytics — newsletter performance
  • SharePoint CRR Weekly Activities list — integrated with Power Automate
  • Google Analytics / GitHub Pages analytics — web tool usage
  • P-Card reconciliation reports — budget tracking
