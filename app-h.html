Appendix H: CRR Annual Calendar

This calendar outlines the annual rhythm of Community Risk Reduction activities organized by fiscal year month (October through September). Use this as a planning guide at the start of each fiscal year to schedule events, order materials, and coordinate with partners. Specific dates vary annually and should be confirmed with the relevant partners and stakeholders. [UPDATE ANNUALLY]

H.1 Monthly Recurring Activities

These activities happen every month regardless of season:

  • CRR Newsletter production and distribution (Constant Contact)
  • ESO community event logging and data maintenance
  • Weekly activity reports to Chief Hedges
  • SharePoint HUB content review
  • Fire truck presentation and station tour requests (as received)
  • Smoke detector installation requests (as received)

H.3 Planning Notes

  • Fire Prevention Week is the single largest CRR effort each year. Begin planning no later than August.
  • Newsletter editorial calendar should be reviewed annually and adjusted based on Fire Marshal incident data and emerging community hazards.
  • Smoke detector installations can spike after major fire incidents. Maintain adequate inventory and Red Cross coordination.
  • Quarterly appraisals follow a consistent structure (see Chapter 14) but the narrative should reflect seasonal program emphasis.
  • Budget year runs October 1 through September 30. FY requests are typically submitted in July-August for the following year.
