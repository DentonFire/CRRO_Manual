Chapter 10

Candidate Pipeline and Outreach

10.1 Recruitment Strategy

The CRR Officer manages a comprehensive recruitment strategy that combines digital outreach, in-person events, and institutional partnerships to build a diverse and qualified candidate pipeline. In Q1 FY 25-26, this strategy generated 402 candidates for the Civil Service Exam.

10.2 Digital Outreach

NEOGOV

NEOGOV is the primary applicant tracking system used for the DFD hiring workflow. The CRR Officer manages all aspects of the NEOGOV platform including:

  • Job posting creation and management
  • Candidate application tracking and PHS processing
  • Hiring workflow stage management (PHS, Polygraph, PAT, Interviews, Conditional Offer, Appointment, DQ Letter)
  • Status updates, candidate communications, and bulk email notifications
  • Reporting and analytics

[ACTION REQUIRED] New CRR Officer must obtain NEOGOV administrator access credentials.

Handshake (UNT)

The CRR Officer maintains a Handshake account through the University of North Texas for posting positions and events to UNT students and alumni. Handshake was finalized in Q1 FY 25-26.

Recruiting Newsletter ("Ready to Serve")

The recruiting newsletter reaches 3,960 subscribers via Constant Contact. It is the primary channel for communicating exam announcements, process updates, PAT prep tips, and general recruiting content. See Chapter 6 for newsletter production details.

Social Media

Recruiting content is posted on DFD social media channels including Instagram (@dentonfiredepartment, \~2,920 followers) and Twitter (@DentonFireDept). Content includes exam announcements, "day in the life" features, career Q\&A sessions (Spaces events), and event promotions. Coordinate with MarComm for brand consistency.

Monthly Webinars & Social Media Spaces

The CRR Officer hosts monthly career Q\&A webinars and social media Spaces events to engage prospective candidates.

10.3 In-Person Recruiting Events

University Partnerships

Partner Activities Key Programs
UNT Career fairs, campus presentations, Handshake postings ELEVATE Athlete recruiting, general career services
TWU Career fairs, recruitment events General career services

Fire Academy Visits

Quarterly visits to fire academies during active recruiting cycles. Target academies include LaGrone Advanced Technology Center (whose graduates receive \+1 preference point), North Central Texas College (NCTC), and Weatherford College.

High School and Middle School Events

Career day presentations at local schools including Guyer High School, Calhoun Middle School, and Strickland Middle School.

Special Recruiting Events

  • UNT ELEVATE Athlete Recruiting \-- Targeted outreach to student athletes
  • Diverse career fairs \-- Events targeting underrepresented demographics
  • Community career events \-- Job fairs and community gatherings

10.4 Diversity Recruitment

Budget: 320100.7826 (Diversity) \-- $20,000 annual

Goal: Workforce reflects community demographics

The demographics report is presented to the Civil Service Commission after each exam. Diversity-focused expenditures are charged to the Diversity budget account.

10.5 Ride-Along Program

The DFD Ride-Along Program provides prospective candidates with firsthand experience of fire department operations. The CRR Officer created a new Ride-Along policy in Q1 FY 25-26 requiring HIPAA training completion prior to participation.

  • HIPAA training required before ride-along participation
  • Candidates complete an application and waiver
  • Coordinate ride-along scheduling with station crews
  • Track participation and candidate feedback
