Preface: How to Use This Manual

This Operations Manual serves as the comprehensive pass-down guide for the Community Risk Reduction (CRR) Officer position at the Denton Fire Department. It is designed to provide a successor with everything needed to assume and effectively manage the full scope of this position, which encompasses both Community Risk Reduction/Public Education and Firefighter Recruitment operations.

The CRR Officer role is unique within the department: a single full-time equivalent (FTE) responsible for 31 distinct program areas spanning community outreach, public education, technology development, the entire Civil Service hiring process, accreditation support, pension board service, and administrative reporting. This manual captures the institutional knowledge, standard operating procedures, vendor relationships, technology systems, and strategic context necessary to maintain continuity across all of these functions.

Document Organization

The manual is organized into the following parts:

  • Part I: Foundation (Chapters 1-3) — Department overview, position scope, and key relationships
  • Part II: Community Risk Reduction Operations (Chapters 4-7) — Public education, newsletters, CRR technology tools, and special programs
  • Part III: Recruitment & Hiring Operations (Chapters 8-11) — Civil Service exam process, candidate pipeline, PAT coordination, and hiring workflow
  • Part IV: Administrative Operations (Chapters 12-15) — Budget management, reporting, accreditation, and pension board duties
  • Part V: Technology & Systems (Chapters 16-17) — All platforms, logins, and automation workflows
  • Part VI: Appendices — Reference materials, templates, contact directory, vendor guide, annual calendars, and supplemental resources

Sections marked with [ACTION REQUIRED] indicate areas where the successor must take specific steps to assume responsibilities (e.g., obtaining system access, updating vendor contacts). Sections marked with [UPDATE ANNUALLY] should be reviewed and refreshed each fiscal year.
