Appendix F

Email Templates

The following email templates are used during the hiring process. Customize as needed for each candidate and cycle. These templates reflect the tone and style established by Captain Lott during FY 25-26.

F.1 VerifEYE Test Link Email

Use this template when sending VerifEYE credibility assessment test links to candidates. Generate the unique link through the VerifEYE/Converus portal before sending.

Field Value
Subject VerifEye Test
To [Candidate email address]
From CRR Officer (Hunter.Lott@cityofdenton.com)

Template body:

[First Name],

Below is your test link. Make sure you follow the instructions completely and listen carefully. The test will ask you various forms of the same question so, if you get one or two messed up, it doesn't mean you fail.

Respectfully,

Captain [Last Name]

[PASTE VERIFEYE LINK HERE]

Notes for this template

  • The link goes AFTER the sign-off at the bottom of the email, not inline in the body
  • The reassuring language about getting one or two questions wrong is intentional and helps reduce candidate anxiety. Do not modify the email template
  • Generate the unique link in the VerifEYE/Converus portal before sending
  • Each candidate gets their own unique link; never reuse links
  • The reassuring language about getting one or two questions wrong is intentional and helps reduce candidate anxiety
  • Remind candidates separately about phone requirements (720p+ camera, 20% battery, Wi-Fi, 6GB storage, quiet room, remove glasses/hats) if not already communicated

F.2 Poly Complete / Investigator Handoff Email

Use this template when notifying background investigators that candidates are completing their VerifEYE exams and are ready for final investigation. This is typically sent when the CRR Officer will be unavailable or when a batch of candidates is progressing simultaneously.

Field Value
Subject Poly Complete
To Background investigators (e.g., Roberts, Miller, Morton)
Cc Stacy Fruge (Laserfiche support)
Bcc Lindsey Garrison (Finance, for awareness)

Template body:

Gents,

[Optional: I'm off on vacation all this coming week. That being said,] As the candidates start completing their VerifEYE exam, I'll [try my best to] move them over to the "PAT" section of the workflow as they come in.

Also, just to make it easier to organize and share the files, I'll ask Stacy (I don't have the ability to do this on my own) to organize both the Poly results and the Pre-Test Questionnaire into their Laserfiche files.

Once you finish your final investigation, please print off their PHS and attach your investigation report on the top with any notes you consider important for the Chiefs to review as they conduct their interviews. Once all final investigations are complete, if someone could drop off all of the packets at my office, I'll lock them away in my secure cabinet until the interviews.

[If unavailable: I'm sure questions will pop up this next week so, feel free to email me. Just know that I probably won't respond until I get back.]

If there's an emergency, please dial 9-1-1.

Respectfully,

Captain [Last Name]

Notes for this template

  • The investigators may request that candidates NOT be notified of advancement until all final investigations are complete
  • Moving candidates in NEOGOV to the PAT stage is an internal tracking action, not a candidate notification
  • Stacy Fruge handles Laserfiche file organization; the CRR Officer does not have direct Laserfiche editing access
  • Investigation packets are physical documents stored in the CRR Officer's secure cabinet
  • The "9-1-1" sign-off is Hunter's signature humor; maintain or replace with your own style

F.3 Candidate Notification Templates

The following are common candidate communication scenarios. These are typically sent via NEOGOV bulk email or individual Outlook messages.

PHS Instructions

Subject: Personal History Statement \- Instructions and Deadline

Body: Congratulations on your exam score. The next step is to complete and submit your Personal History Statement (PHS). Access the form here: [Laserfiche link]. The deadline for submission is [DATE]. Incomplete or late packets will not be accepted. Contact me with any questions.

Practice PAT Notification

Subject: Practice PAT Session \- [DATE]

Body: You are invited to attend a Practice PAT session at Station 7 on [DATE] at [TIME]. This is an opportunity to familiarize yourself with the course and equipment before the official PAT. Dress in athletic clothing and bring water. The session is optional but strongly recommended.

Practice PAT Reschedule

Subject: UPDATED: Practice PAT Rescheduled to [NEW DATE]

Body: Due to [weather conditions / facility issue], the Practice PAT originally scheduled for [OLD DATE] has been rescheduled to [NEW DATE] at [TIME] at Station 7\. All other details remain the same. Apologies for the inconvenience.

VerifEYE Pre-Test Questionnaire Distribution

Subject: Pre-Test Questionnaire \- Action Required

Body: Attached is the DFD Pre-Test Questionnaire. Please complete all sections thoroughly and return the completed form to me by [DATE]. This is a required step before your credibility assessment. Honesty is critical. Disclosing information does not automatically disqualify you, but falsification or omission may result in permanent disqualification.

DQ Notification (Cover Email)

Subject: Denton Fire Department \- Application Status

Body: Please see the attached letter regarding your application status with the Denton Fire Department. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact me.
