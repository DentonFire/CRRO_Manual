6.3 Newsletter Production Workflow

  1. Topic selection — Review incident data and seasonal hazard calendar. Identify the primary risk topic for the month.
  1. Content development — Research the topic, gather statistics (national and local), develop 3-5 actionable safety tips.
  1. Draft in Constant Contact — Follow the standard template structure (see below). Include DFD branding and images.
  1. Review — Proofread for accuracy, check all links, verify statistics.
  1. Send — Schedule or send the newsletter. Typical send day: first week of the month.
  1. Track analytics — After 48 hours, review open rates, click-through rates, and engagement.
  1. Archive link — Save the Constant Contact archive URL for quarterly report appendix.

Standard Newsletter Structure

  • Opening hook — Seasonal or timely connection to grab reader attention
  • Main safety topic — 3-5 specific, actionable recommendations
  • Supporting statistics — National data to contextualize the risk
  • DFD resources mention — Link to relevant tools, programs, or services
  • Call to action — Share the newsletter, contact DFD, attend an event, or access a resource

6.4 Recruiting Newsletter — "Ready to Serve" Series

The recruiting newsletter is distributed as needed during active hiring cycles. Content focuses on:

  • Official exam announcements and registration deadlines
  • Hiring process roadmap and step-by-step guidance
  • PAT preparation tips and training resources
  • Ride-along program information and eligibility
  • Extra points reminders and required documentation
  • Starting salary and benefits information ($86,022 starting salary)

6.5 Constant Contact Administration

The CRR Officer is the sole administrator of the DFD Constant Contact account. Key administrative tasks include:

  • Managing subscriber lists (separate lists for CRR and Recruiting audiences)
  • Creating and scheduling email campaigns
  • Monitoring deliverability, bounce rates, and spam complaints
  • Maintaining compliance with CAN-SPAM requirements (unsubscribe functionality, physical address, sender identification)
  • Exporting analytics data for quarterly reports
  • Archiving newsletter links for appendices (archive URLs follow format: conta.cc/XXXXXXX)

[ACTION REQUIRED] New CRR Officer must obtain Constant Contact login credentials and be added as account administrator.
