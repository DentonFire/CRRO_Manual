12.3 P-Card Procedures

The CRR Officer uses a departmental P-Card (purchasing card) for approved expenditures. The P-Card reconciliation process must be completed regularly:

  1. Make purchase — Ensure the expenditure is within budget and properly authorized
  1. Save receipt — Retain physical or digital receipt for every transaction
  1. Log expense — Record in the CRR Expenses SharePoint list with vendor, amount, account code, and description
  1. Reconcile in Emburse — Complete P-Card reconciliation through the Emburse system, assigning each transaction to the correct budget account
  1. Submit — Complete reconciliation before the monthly deadline
