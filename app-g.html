G.2 VerifEYE / Converus — Credibility Assessment

Overview

VerifEYE is an eye-tracking based credibility assessment that replaced traditional polygraph testing for DFD candidate screening. The system measures involuntary eye behavior changes when a subject is being deceptive.

  • Test Version: V4R (4 topics)
  • Test ID (No Admissions): 3843
  • Delivery: Solo Mode (candidate completes remotely on mobile device) or Proctored (in-person)
  • Dashboard: Converus admin portal — generates test links, displays results
  • MFA: Microsoft Authenticator on CRR Officer work phone

Test Types

  • No Admissions: Standard test for candidates whose Pre-Test Questionnaire shows no disqualifying admissions ("clean" questionnaire)
  • Admissions: Used when candidate disclosed issues on the Pre-Test Questionnaire. Verifies the completeness and truthfulness of disclosures.

Four Test Topics (V4R)

  1. Illegal drug use within the past 5 years
  2. Employment termination (fired, resigned to avoid firing, or asked to resign)
  3. Major criminal activity
  4. Terrorism associations

Workflow

  1. Review candidate's Pre-Test Questionnaire responses
  2. Determine test type (No Admissions vs. Admissions) based on four trigger categories
  3. Log into Converus dashboard (credentials in secure credentials document)
  4. Generate test link for appropriate test type
  5. Email link to candidate using template (Appendix F.1)
  6. Monitor dashboard for completion and results
  7. Document results and advance/disqualify in NEOGOV

G.3 FPSI — Civil Service Exam

Fire & Police Selection, Inc. (FPSI) provides the written Civil Service exam for firefighter hiring. The CRR Officer coordinates with FPSI for:

  • Exam material ordering and delivery logistics
  • Score processing and eligibility list generation
  • Preference point application
  • Exam security and proctoring requirements

Exams are typically scheduled per hiring cycle. Coordinate scheduling at least 8-10 weeks in advance. FPSI provides exam booklets, answer sheets, and scoring services. Results are returned to the CRR Officer who applies preference points and generates the ranked eligibility list.

G.4 Concentra — Pre-Employment Medical

Concentra provides occupational health services for conditional hires:

  • NFPA 1582-compliant medical examination
  • Drug screening
  • Hearing and vision testing
  • Pulmonary function testing

Cost: ~$825 per candidate (charged to 320100.7726)

Location: Denton area Concentra clinic

Scheduling: CRR Officer schedules appointments after conditional letter of employment

G.5 Pepper & Associates — Psychological Evaluation

Pepper & Associates provides pre-employment psychological evaluations:

  • NFPA 1582 psychological screening
  • Candidate assessment report provided to CRR Officer

Cost: ~$215 per candidate (charged to 320100.7726)

Scheduling: CRR Officer schedules after conditional letter of employment, typically same week as medical

G.6 Constant Contact — Newsletter Platform

The CRR Officer is the sole administrator of the DFD Constant Contact account.

  • CRR Newsletter: Monthly, 471 subscribers (as of Q1 FY 25-26)
  • Recruiting Newsletter ("Ready to Serve"): As-needed during hiring cycles, 3,960 subscribers

Key administrative tasks: managing subscriber lists, creating/scheduling campaigns, monitoring analytics (deliverability, bounce rates, spam complaints), maintaining CAN-SPAM compliance, exporting data for quarterly reports, archiving newsletter links.

[ACTION REQUIRED] New CRR Officer must obtain Constant Contact login credentials from the secure credentials document and be added as account administrator.

G.7 DFD Pre-Test Questionnaire

The DFD Pre-Test Questionnaire is a fillable PDF maintained by the CRR Officer. The current version (February 2026) contains 10 sections covering: drug use, employment termination, criminal activity, terrorism, honesty/integrity, prior fire/LE service, alcohol, other applications, prostitution (with military overseas exception), and a certification/signature page.

The fillable PDF is stored on the SharePoint HUB. Update the questionnaire if Hiring Guidelines are revised or if VerifEYE test topics change.
