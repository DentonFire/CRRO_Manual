A.4 Hiring Process Quick Reference

Seven-step firefighter hiring process under TX Local Government Code Chapter 143:

Step Phase Vendor/Owner Key Details
1 Civil Service Exam FPSI Written test; aim for 85+ score; preference points applied
2 Personal History Statement CRR Officer Background documentation packet via NEOGOV
3 Polygraph VerifEYE Remote mobile app; replaced traditional polygraph
4 Physical Ability Test CRR \+ Truck 1 Timed physical assessment; prep sessions offered
5 Panel Interviews Chief-level panel Interview process developed with Chief Skipper
6 Conditional Offer CRR Officer Medical (\~$825 Concentra), Psych (\~$215 Pepper)
7 Appointment Letter CRR Officer Final hiring offer; coordinate start date
